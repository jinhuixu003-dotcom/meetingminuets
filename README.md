# MeetingMinutes – AI Meeting Recorder, Transcription & Meeting Minutes

> AI-powered meeting recorder with real-time transcription, speaker recognition, meeting summaries, translation, mind maps, PowerPoint generation, and cloud collaboration.

MeetingMinutes is an AI meeting assistant designed for professionals, students, journalists, researchers, legal teams, healthcare workers, and enterprises.

It helps you record meetings, transcribe speech into text, generate meeting minutes, summarize discussions, translate multilingual conversations, and automatically organize knowledge—all in one app.

---

# Why MeetingMinutes?

MeetingMinutes is more than a voice recorder.

It transforms conversations into searchable, editable, and shareable knowledge.

Whether you're attending an online meeting, offline conference, interview, lecture, customer visit, or brainstorming session, MeetingMinutes automatically captures every important detail.
