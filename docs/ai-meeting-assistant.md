# AI Meeting Assistant: What It Is and How It Can Improve Meetings

## What Is an AI Meeting Assistant?

An AI meeting assistant is a software tool that uses artificial intelligence to help people record, transcribe, summarize, translate, organize, and collaborate on meeting content.

Instead of manually taking notes during a meeting, an AI meeting assistant can capture conversations, convert speech into text, identify different speakers, generate meeting summaries, and organize important information automatically.

AI meeting assistants are commonly used for:

* Business meetings
* Team meetings
* Online meetings
* Client meetings
* Interviews
* Lectures
* Conferences
* Research
* Medical discussions
* Legal meetings
* Sales calls
* Project discussions

The main goal is to reduce the amount of manual note-taking required during meetings and make important information easier to find and use later.
