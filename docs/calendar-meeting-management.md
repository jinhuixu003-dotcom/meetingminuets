# Calendar Meeting Management: Organize and Find Recorded Meetings with MeetingMinutes

## What Is Meeting Calendar Management?

Meeting calendar management is the process of organizing, finding, and reviewing meetings based on dates and meeting history.

When users record multiple meetings every week, it can become difficult to remember:

* When a meeting took place
* Which meeting was recorded on a specific date
* Where a particular recording is stored
* Which transcript belongs to which meeting
* When an important discussion happened

MeetingMinutes provides calendar-based meeting management that allows users to view and organize meeting content by date.

Combined with cloud synchronization, keyword search, and file management, the calendar feature helps users manage their recorded meetings more efficiently.

---

# MeetingMinutes Calendar

MeetingMinutes includes a calendar feature that allows users to view meetings based on dates.

Users can check the meetings associated with a specific day and quickly locate relevant meeting content.

A simple workflow is:

```text
Calendar
    ↓

    ↓
View Meetings on That Day
    ↓
Select a Meeting
    ↓
Review Recording
    ↓
View Transcript
    ↓
Review Meeting Notes
```

This provides a chronological way to manage meeting history.
