# AI PPT Generation from Meeting Recordings and Notes with MeetingMinutes

## What Is AI PPT Generation?

AI PPT generation helps users turn meeting content, recordings, transcripts, and structured information into presentation materials.

Creating a presentation manually can take significant time.

Users may need to:

* Review meeting recordings
* Read transcripts
* Extract key information
* Organize topics
* Create an outline
* Write slide content
* Build presentation pages
* Format the final presentation

MeetingMinutes provides an AI-powered workflow that can help turn meeting content into structured presentation materials.

The workflow can be:

```text id="m7q3x9"
Meeting Recording
        ↓
Speech-to-Text
        ↓
Transcript
        ↓
AI Content Analysis
        ↓
Key Points
        ↓
Presentation Structure
        ↓
PPT Generation
```

This can reduce the time required to turn meeting information into a presentation.
