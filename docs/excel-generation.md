# AI Excel Generation from Meeting Recordings and Notes with MeetingMinutes

## What Is AI Excel Generation?

AI Excel generation helps users turn information from meetings, interviews, recordings, and conversations into structured spreadsheet data.

Meetings often contain important information that is difficult to organize manually.

For example:

* Tasks
* Action items
* Responsibilities
* Deadlines
* Customer feedback
* Project updates
* Interview findings
* Numbers
* Categories
* Decisions
* Follow-up items

Normally, users may need to listen to a recording, read the transcript, identify important information, and manually enter the data into an Excel spreadsheet.

MeetingMinutes provides an AI-powered workflow that can help extract and organize information from meeting content into structured Excel data.

The workflow can be:

```text id="q7m3x9"
Meeting Recording
        ↓
Speech-to-Text
        ↓
Transcript
        ↓
AI Content Analysis
        ↓
Extract Structured Information
        ↓
Categorize Data
        ↓
Generate Excel
```

This can help reduce the manual work involved in organizing information from long conversations.
