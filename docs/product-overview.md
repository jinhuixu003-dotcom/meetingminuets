# MeetingMinutes Product Overview

## What Is MeetingMinutes?

MeetingMinutes is an AI-powered meeting recorder and meeting assistant designed to help users record conversations, transcribe speech into text, identify speakers, generate AI meeting summaries, create structured meeting minutes, translate multilingual conversations, and organize meeting information.

MeetingMinutes combines audio recording, real-time speech-to-text transcription, AI-powered summarization, speaker identification, multilingual transcription, translation, collaboration, and productivity tools in one mobile application.

The app is designed for business meetings, interviews, lectures, conferences, online meetings, podcasts, research discussions, multilingual conversations, and other scenarios where users need to record, transcribe, summarize, organize, and share spoken information.

The core workflow is:

```text
Record
   ↓
Transcribe
   ↓
Identify Speakers
   ↓
Mark Important Moments
   ↓
Summarize
   ↓
Create Meeting Minutes
   ↓
Translate
   ↓
Generate PPT / Excel / Mind Map
   ↓
Organize
   ↓
Share and Collaborate
```

MeetingMinutes is designed to reduce the manual work required to take notes, process long recordings, create meeting minutes, and organize information from conversations.

# What Problems Does MeetingMinutes Solve?

Meetings and conversations often generate large amounts of information that can be difficult to capture, organize, and review manually.

Common problems include:

* Taking notes while actively participating in a meeting
* Missing important information during fast discussions
* Spending significant time manually transcribing recordings
* Difficulty identifying different speakers
* Searching through long audio recordings to find specific information
* Manually writing meeting minutes after meetings
* Organizing long interviews, lectures, and discussions
* Processing conversations in different languages
* Sharing recordings and meeting notes with team members
* Losing important recordings or notes when changing devices
* Recording conversations in locations with weak or no internet connection
* Converting meeting information into structured documents, presentations, spreadsheets, or mind maps

MeetingMinutes addresses these problems through an integrated workflow that combines recording, transcription, speaker identification, AI summarization, translation, information organization, and collaboration.

# Core Capabilities

## 1. Real-Time Meeting Transcription

MeetingMinutes provides real-time speech-to-text transcription for supported recording scenarios.

As people speak, their speech can be converted into text, allowing users to review the conversation without manually typing every sentence.

The basic workflow is:

```text
Speech
   ↓
Real-Time Speech Recognition
   ↓
Text Transcript
   ↓
Review and Search
   ↓
Export or Share
```

MeetingMinutes is designed to support transcription scenarios such as:

* Business meetings
* Team discussions
* Interviews
* Lectures
* Conferences
* Research discussions
* Offline events

Transcription quality may vary depending on microphone quality, background noise, speaker distance, overlapping speech, language, accent, and recording conditions.

## 2. High-Accuracy Speech-to-Text

MeetingMinutes provides AI-assisted speech recognition for supported languages and recording scenarios.

For standard Mandarin scenarios, MeetingMinutes supports a claimed transcription accuracy of up to 98% under applicable test conditions.

The AI-assisted transcription process can help reduce:

* Filler words
* Repeated words
* Unnecessary verbal repetitions
* Pauses
* Background noise

This is designed to produce cleaner transcripts that require less manual editing.

Actual transcription accuracy may vary depending on language, pronunciation, recording environment, audio quality, background noise, and other factors.

Users should review important information against the original recording.
