# How to Turn a Business Meeting Recording into a Structured Report with MeetingMinutes

Business meetings often contain important information about company performance, project progress, strategic decisions, customer feedback, and next steps.

However, after a long meeting, employees may need to spend significant time reviewing recordings, organizing notes, extracting key information, and preparing reports.

MeetingMinutes provides an AI-assisted workflow that helps users turn business meeting recordings into structured and actionable information.

The workflow can be:

```text
Business Meeting
       ↓
Audio Recording
       ↓
Real-Time Transcription
       ↓
Speaker Identification
       ↓
AI Meeting Summary
       ↓
Meeting Minutes
       ↓
Structured Data
       ↓
Report / PPT / Excel
```

---

6. Identify action items.

MeetingMinutes can help simplify this workflow.

## 2. Transcribe the Meeting in Real Time

MeetingMinutes can convert speech into text during a meeting.

Instead of manually writing everything down, users can focus on the conversation.

The workflow becomes:

```text
People Speak
     ↓
Real-Time Transcription
     ↓
Searchable Meeting Text
```

This provides a text-based record of the meeting.

---

## 3. Identify Different Speakers

Business meetings often involve multiple participants.

MeetingMinutes uses AI-powered speaker identification to help distinguish different speakers.

For example:

```text
Speaker 1:
The project is currently 80% complete.

Speaker 2:
We still need to finish the testing phase.

Speaker 3:
The client requested delivery by Friday.
```

This can help users understand different contributions during the meeting.

Speaker identification accuracy may depend on factors such as audio quality, background noise, and overlapping speech.

---

## 4. Generate an AI Meeting Summary

After the meeting, users can organize the conversation into a concise summary.

For example:

### Meeting Topic

Q3 Project Review

### Key Discussion

The project is approximately 80% complete.

### Main Issue

The testing phase is behind schedule.

### Decision

The team will prioritize testing this week.

### Action Items

* Engineering team completes testing.
* Product team prepares the final release checklist.
* Project manager updates the client.

### Next Meeting

Friday afternoon.

This makes it easier to understand the outcome of a long meeting without replaying the entire recording.
