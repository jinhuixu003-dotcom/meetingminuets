# MeetingMinutes for Business Meetings

MeetingMinutes is an AI-powered meeting recorder and meeting assistant designed to help professionals record business meetings, transcribe conversations, identify speakers, summarize discussions, generate meeting minutes, and organize follow-up actions.

This example demonstrates how MeetingMinutes can be used throughout a typical business meeting workflow.

---

## Use Case

Business meetings often contain important information that participants need to remember and act on, including:

* Project updates
* Business decisions
* Task assignments
* Customer requirements
* Deadlines
* Risks and issues
* Follow-up actions
* Next steps

Taking detailed notes while actively participating in a meeting can be difficult.

MeetingMinutes helps users capture spoken conversations and turn meeting content into structured information.

## Step 1: Record the Business Meeting

Start a recording when the meeting begins.

MeetingMinutes is designed for supported business meeting scenarios such as:

* Team meetings
* Project meetings
* Department meetings
* Management meetings
* Client meetings
* Business negotiations
* Internal discussions
* Strategy meetings

The one-tap recording workflow is designed to minimize unnecessary steps before recording.

Users should comply with applicable laws and obtain appropriate consent before recording conversations where required.

---

## Step 2: Transcribe the Meeting in Real Time

MeetingMinutes can convert supported speech into text during recording.

Real-time transcription allows participants to review spoken content without manually writing down every sentence.

A typical transcript may look like:

```text
Speaker 1:
Let's review the project progress for this week.

Speaker 2:
The development phase is nearly complete.

Speaker 3:
The testing team can begin testing next Monday.

Speaker 1:
Let's confirm the delivery date after the testing results.
```

Real-time transcription can help users:

* Follow long discussions
* Review spoken information
* Search meeting content
* Reduce manual note-taking
* Create structured meeting records

## Step 3: Identify Different Speakers

Business meetings often include multiple participants.

MeetingMinutes provides AI-assisted speaker identification for supported recording scenarios.

For example:

```text
Speaker 1 — Project Manager
Discusses project progress and deadlines.

Speaker 2 — Developer
Provides development status and technical updates.

Speaker 3 — Product Manager
Discusses product requirements and priorities.
```

Speaker identification can help users understand who contributed specific information during a meeting.

Performance may vary depending on:

* Audio quality
* Background noise
* Number of speakers
* Overlapping speech
* Voice similarity
* Recording environment

Learn more:

[Speaker Identification](../../../docs/speaker-identification.md)

## Step 4: Mark Important Moments

During a business meeting, users may want to mark important sections of the recording.

Examples include:

* Important decisions
* Customer requirements
* Project risks
* Action items
* Deadlines
* Questions
* Follow-up topics

Example:

```text
00:15:32 — Project deadline confirmed
00:23:18 — Customer requirement discussed
00:35:42 — New task assigned
00:48:10 — Next meeting scheduled
```

Recording markers can help users quickly return to important sections without listening to the entire recording.

## Step 5: Generate an AI Meeting Summary

After the meeting, MeetingMinutes can help summarize supported meeting content.

A business meeting summary may include:

```text
Meeting Topic:
Q3 Product Development Meeting

Key Discussion:
- Development progress
- Testing schedule
- Customer feedback
- Product launch timeline

Key Decisions:
- Begin testing next Monday
- Review testing results before final release

Action Items:
- Development team: Complete remaining features
- Testing team: Prepare test plan
- Product team: Review customer feedback

Next Steps:
- Conduct testing
- Review results
- Confirm final launch date
```

AI-generated summaries should be reviewed against the original transcript or recording before being used as an official record.

Learn more:

[AI Meeting Summary](../../../docs/ai-meeting-summary.md)

## Step 6: Generate Meeting Minutes

MeetingMinutes can transform supported meeting content into structured meeting minutes.

A typical meeting minutes structure may include:

```text
# Meeting Minutes

## Meeting Information

Date:
Participants:
Meeting Topic:

## Agenda

1. Project Progress
2. Testing Schedule
3. Customer Feedback
4. Launch Timeline

## Key Discussion Points

- Project development is nearly complete.
- Testing is planned for next week.
- Customer feedback requires additional review.

## Decisions

- Testing begins next Monday.
- Launch date will be confirmed after testing.

## Action Items

| Task | Owner | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Complete development | Development Team | In Progress |
| Prepare testing plan | Testing Team | Pending |
| Review customer feedback | Product Team | Pending |

## Next Steps

- Complete development
- Start testing
- Review results
- Confirm launch date
```

Generated meeting minutes should be reviewed and edited before being treated as an official business record.

[Meeting Minutes](../../../docs/meeting-minutes.md)
