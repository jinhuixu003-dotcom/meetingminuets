# How to Record, Transcribe, and Summarize Medical Meetings with MeetingMinutes

Medical professionals often participate in meetings, academic discussions, medical conferences, training sessions, research interviews, and multidisciplinary case discussions.

These conversations can contain a large amount of detailed information that is difficult to capture through manual note-taking alone.

MeetingMinutes provides an AI-assisted workflow for recording, transcribing, summarizing, and organizing spoken content in supported scenarios.

The workflow can be:

```text
Medical Meeting
      ↓
Audio Recording
      ↓
Speech-to-Text
      ↓
Speaker Identification
      ↓
AI Summary
      ↓
Structured Notes
      ↓
Search / Review / Share
```

MeetingMinutes is designed to help organize spoken information. It is not a medical diagnosis or clinical decision-making system.

Users should follow applicable healthcare privacy, data protection, institutional, and professional requirements when recording or processing medical information.
