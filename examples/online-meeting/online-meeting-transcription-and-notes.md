# How to Record, Transcribe, and Summarize Online Meetings with MeetingMinutes

Online meetings have become an essential part of modern work.

Remote teams, distributed companies, freelancers, consultants, and international organizations often use online meetings to discuss projects, make decisions, solve problems, and coordinate work.

However, it can be difficult to participate in an online meeting while simultaneously taking detailed notes.

MeetingMinutes provides an AI-assisted workflow that can help users record conversations, transcribe speech, identify speakers, summarize discussions, and organize meeting information.

The workflow can be:

```text
Online Meeting
      ↓
Audio Recording
      ↓
Real-Time Transcription
      ↓
Speaker Identification
      ↓
AI Meeting Summary
      ↓
Meeting Minutes
      ↓
Action Items
      ↓
Search / Review / Share
```

This can help turn an online meeting from a temporary conversation into a searchable and reusable record.

Meeting minutes organize the most important information from a meeting.

A typical meeting minutes structure can include:


```text

- Sales Team

## Key Discussion
Launch schedule and marketing preparation.

## Decisions

## Action Items
- Product: Complete testing.
- Marketing: Prepare campaign.
- Sales: Update customer materials.
```

This can make meeting information easier to share with people who attended or missed the meeting.
