# How to Transcribe, Summarize, and Organize Podcasts with MeetingMinutes

Podcasts often contain long-form conversations, interviews, discussions, and expert insights.

A single podcast episode may last 30 minutes, 1 hour, or several hours, making it difficult to listen to the entire episode when you only need specific information.

MeetingMinutes provides an AI-assisted workflow that can help users record, import, transcribe, summarize, translate, and organize podcast audio and video content.

The workflow can be:

```text id="q7m3x8"
Podcast Recording
       ↓
Audio / Video Import
       ↓
Speech-to-Text
       ↓
Speaker Identification
       ↓
AI Summary
       ↓
Key Topics
       ↓
Searchable Transcript
       ↓
Structured Content
```

This can help turn long podcast episodes into searchable and reusable text content.

# Why Use MeetingMinutes for Podcast Transcription?

Podcasts contain valuable information, but audio is difficult to search and organize.

MeetingMinutes helps turn podcast conversations into reusable digital content through a workflow that connects:

**Record or Import → Transcribe → Identify Speakers → Search → Mark Important Moments → Summarize → Organize → Export**

For researchers, content creators, journalists, students, and business professionals, this can make long podcast episodes easier to understand, search, analyze, and reuse.

MeetingMinutes helps transform podcast audio from a passive listening experience into a searchable and structured knowledge resource.
