# How to Record, Transcribe, and Summarize Interviews with MeetingMinutes

Interviews often contain valuable information, but reviewing a long interview recording and turning it into useful notes can take a significant amount of time.

Whether you are conducting a customer interview, user research interview, market research interview, expert interview, or job interview, MeetingMinutes provides an AI-assisted workflow to help record conversations, transcribe speech, identify speakers, summarize key information, and organize interview results.

The workflow can be:

```text
Interview
    ↓
Audio Recording
    ↓
Real-Time Transcription
    ↓
Speaker Identification
    ↓
AI Summary
    ↓
Key Findings
    ↓
Structured Notes
    ↓
Excel / PPT / Mind Map
```

This can help users spend less time manually organizing interview recordings and more time analyzing the information collected.

# Step 3: Identify Different Speakers

Interviews normally involve at least two people.

For example:

```text
Interviewer:
What is the biggest problem you have with the current product?

Participant:
The biggest issue is that it takes too long to find information.
```

Speaker identification can help organize the conversation.

A transcript may look like:

```text
Speaker 1:
What is the biggest problem?

Speaker 2:
It takes too long to find information.

Speaker 1:
How often does this happen?

Speaker 2:
Almost every day.
```

This makes it easier to distinguish questions from answers.

Speaker identification performance can depend on audio quality, background noise, overlapping speech, and other recording conditions.

# Step 4: Generate an AI Interview Summary

Long interview transcripts can contain thousands of words.

Instead of reading the entire transcript every time, users can organize the conversation into a concise summary.

For example:

### Interview Topic

User experience with a project management application.

### Main Pain Point

The participant finds it difficult to locate older project information.

### Current Workflow

The participant manually searches through multiple documents.

### Requested Improvement

The participant wants faster keyword search and better organization.

### Key Insight

Information retrieval is one of the biggest usability problems.

This summary can help researchers quickly understand the main findings.

AI-generated summaries should be reviewed against the original transcript before being used as formal research findings.
