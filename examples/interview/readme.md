# MeetingMinutes for Interviews

MeetingMinutes is an AI-powered meeting recorder and meeting assistant that can help users record interviews, transcribe conversations, identify speakers, mark important moments, summarize interview content, and organize interview notes.

This example demonstrates how MeetingMinutes can be used throughout an interview workflow, from recording the conversation to creating structured interview notes.

---

# Interview Use Cases

Interviews can generate a large amount of spoken information.

Whether conducting a job interview, user research interview, journalistic interview, expert interview, or academic research interview, manually taking notes while actively participating in the conversation can cause important information to be missed.

MeetingMinutes helps users capture interview conversations and turn spoken content into structured information.

Common interview scenarios include:

* Job interviews
* Recruitment interviews
* User research interviews
* Customer interviews
* Journalistic interviews
* Expert interviews
* Academic research interviews
* Market research interviews
* Podcast interviews
* Field interviews

# 1. Record the Interview

Start recording when the interview begins.

MeetingMinutes can be used for supported interview scenarios including:

* Face-to-face interviews
* Remote interviews
* Professional interviews
* Research interviews
* Customer interviews
* Expert interviews
* Journalistic interviews

The recording workflow is designed to make it easier to focus on the conversation instead of manually writing down every detail.

Users should comply with applicable laws and obtain appropriate consent before recording interviews or conversations where required.

# 2. Transcribe the Interview in Real Time

MeetingMinutes can convert supported speech into text during recording.

Real-time transcription can help interviewers and researchers review spoken content while the conversation is taking place or shortly afterward.

Example:

```text id="b0v8am"
Interviewer:
Can you tell us about your experience with the product?

Interviewee:
I started using it about six months ago. The feature I use most often is the transcription function.

Interviewer:
What problems did you have before using it?

Interviewee:
I used to take notes manually, which meant I often missed parts of the conversation.
```

Real-time transcription can help users:

* Review interview conversations
* Search for specific information
* Reduce manual note-taking
* Identify important statements
* Create structured interview records

# 3. Identify Interview Speakers

Most interviews involve at least two speakers.

MeetingMinutes provides AI-assisted speaker identification for supported multi-speaker recordings.

Example:

```text id="j9w1v2"
Speaker 1 — Interviewer

Can you describe your experience with the product?

Speaker 2 — Interviewee

I started using it about six months ago.

Speaker 1 — Interviewer

What feature do you use most often?

Speaker 2 — Interviewee

The transcription feature is the most useful for me.
```

Speaker identification can help users distinguish:

* Interviewer
* Interviewee
* Multiple interview participants
* Panel members
* Research participants

Speaker identification performance may vary depending on:

* Audio quality
* Background noise
* Number of speakers
* Overlapping speech
* Voice similarity
* Recording environment

[Speaker Identification](../../../docs/speaker-identification.md)

# 5. Generate an AI Interview Summary

After an interview, MeetingMinutes can help summarize supported interview content.

An AI-generated interview summary may organize information into sections such as:

```text id="q8a5de"
Interview Overview

Interview Type:
Customer Interview

Main Topics:
- Product usage
- User experience
- Pain points
- Feature requests

Key Findings:
- User frequently uses transcription.
- Manual note-taking was previously time-consuming.
- The user wants faster access to important information.

Pain Points:
- Difficult to take notes while talking
- Difficult to review long recordings

Feature Requests:
- Better organization of interview content
- Faster access to important sections

Follow-Up:
- Conduct additional user interviews
- Investigate requested features
```

AI-generated summaries should be reviewed against the original interview transcript or recording.

# Privacy and Responsible Use

Interview recordings and transcripts may contain personal, confidential, or sensitive information.

Users should:

* Obtain appropriate consent before recording interviews where required
* Follow applicable privacy and data protection laws
* Protect confidential interview information
* Avoid sharing recordings with unauthorized people
* Review AI-generated summaries before relying on them
* Verify important information against the original recording or transcript

For employment interviews, AI-generated information should not be used as the sole basis for hiring or employment decisions.

For journalism and research, users should verify important statements and quotations against the original recording.

# Summary

MeetingMinutes can support interview workflows by combining:

**Record → Transcribe → Identify Speakers → Mark Important Moments → Summarize → Organize → Export → Share**

It can be used for job interviews, user research, customer interviews, journalistic interviews, expert interviews, academic research, and multilingual interviews.

The goal is to help users turn long interview conversations into structured, searchable, and actionable information while reducing manual transcription and note-taking work.
